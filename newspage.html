What the workers have to say!

I was just so confused Says Waitress Emily, who just started her shift before the Bear strolled in. I was wiping tables and I look behind me and see a huge bear just staring at me! All I could do was freeze and hope he would go away! One of the scariest days of my Life!

Why was the bear in the Diner?

Scientists have shown studies of abnormal activities of American Black bears in recent years around Colorado, which is where the diner is located. We Haven't figured out the cause of these abnormalizes in the bears recent attitude, but we seem to come to the conclusion that they are becoming more social creatures and among the years grown to get used to humans without much fear or worry States Mr. Malcov, Scientist and researcher at Colorado wildlife sanctuary.
